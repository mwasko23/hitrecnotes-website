Having trouble?

Download won't start or gets stuck

Try right-clicking the download button and selecting "Save link as...". If using Chrome, check chrome://downloads and click Resume. You can also try a different browser.

Windows says "Windows protected your PC"

This is normal for new apps. Click "More info" (blue text below the message), then click "Run anyway". The app is safe to install.

Antivirus is blocking the installer

Some antivirus programs flag new apps. Temporarily disable your antivirus, install Hit Rec Notes, then re-enable it. You can also add an exception for the installer.
